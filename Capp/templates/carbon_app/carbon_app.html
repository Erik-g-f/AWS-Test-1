Carbon App Calculator

Our app empowers you to reduce your carbon emissions by delivering actionable insights into your transportation choices. It offers a way to log your carbon-emitting trips and provides personal footprint comparisons to help you make smarter, more sustainable decisions. You can also use the quick-log function to help you quickly log recurring trips.

Instructions
  • Select a transport mode from the home screen (Car, Motorbike, Bus, Train, Plane, or Ferry).
  • Enter the number of kilometers traveled.
  • Select vehicle type or energy source if required (e.g., gasoline, electric).
  • Specify the number of passengers (including yourself).
  • If you want to reuse the entry later, check "Save this trip?" and give it a name.
  • Click "Submit" to log the data and view your carbon emissions for that trip.
